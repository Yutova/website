Your privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

Yutova is designed to be a private space for reflection. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and the choices available to you.

Information we collect

We may collect account information you provide, such as your email address, along with information necessary to operate and secure the service. Your journal entries, recordings, and reflections are treated as personal content.

How we use information

We use information to:

  • provide, maintain, and improve Yutova;
  • generate reflections in response to your entries;
  • respond to support requests and communicate service updates; and
  • protect the safety, security, and integrity of the service.

Your journal content

Your journal content is private. We do not sell it or use it to train public or shared AI models. We only process it as needed to provide your requested Yutova experience.

Sharing information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with service providers that help us operate Yutova, where they are bound to handle it only for those purposes. We may also disclose information when required by law or to protect people and the service.

Your choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, subject to applicable law. To make a request, contact us at hello@yutova.app.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we do, we will post the revised version here and update the date above.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to hello@yutova.app.
